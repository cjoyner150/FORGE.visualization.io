The descending line
Each spin without a win costs $1. The slope of the descent between dots is the dry spell made visible — not as absence, but as duration. A histogram cannot show this.
The dots
Win events sized by payout magnitude. A small dot is a Scrap or Shatter match. A large dot is a wild-amplified outcome. A large orange dot during a surge window is the session-defining moment.
The before
A 28× win after 3 spins feels different from a 28× win after 40 spins. The payout is identical. The emotional weight is not. This visualization makes the before visible alongside the win.
